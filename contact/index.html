02 — Reporting an issue

Found a bug? Tell us in detail.

The more context you can include, the faster we can fix it. When reporting an issue with one of our apps, please share:

  • The app you’re using and the affected store domain.
  • What you were trying to do.
  • What actually happened (screenshots help a lot).
  • The browser and device you were using.
  • The approximate time it occurred (so we can match it to logs).
